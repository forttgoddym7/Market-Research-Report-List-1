<p><p>End-Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) device market comprises three main types: Hemodialysis, Peritoneal Dialysis, and Kidney Transplantation. Hemodialysis uses a machine to filter waste from the blood, typically performed in-clinic or at home. Peritoneal Dialysis involves filling the abdominal cavity with a cleansing solution to remove waste, allowing for greater flexibility in home care. Kidney Transplantation replaces the diseased kidney with a healthy donor kidney, offering a potential cure but facing challenges like donor availability and immunosuppression management.</p></p>
