<p><p>Cloud computing encompasses several market types: Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, enabling users to manage and scale their IT infrastructure without physical hardware. Platform as a Service (PaaS) offers a development environment for building, testing, and deploying applications, simplifying the software development process. Software as a Service (SaaS) delivers software applications via the cloud, allowing users to access and use them on-demand without installation, enhancing convenience and collaboration across devices.</p></p>
