<p><p>The polyelectrolyte market is categorized into three main types: polysalts, polycations, and polyanions. Polysalts are neutral polymers that form when polycations and polyanions combine, often used in wastewater treatment and flocculation. Polycations are positively charged polymers, utilized in applications like drug delivery and water purification. Polyanions are negatively charged polymers, commonly employed in soil stabilization and as dispersants. Each type serves distinct roles across various industries, driven by their unique chemical properties and interactions.</p></p>
