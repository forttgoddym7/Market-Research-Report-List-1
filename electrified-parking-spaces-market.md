<p><p>Electrified parking spaces can be categorized into two primary market types: single-system electrification and dual-system electrification. Single-system electrification focuses on providing power to electric vehicles through a dedicated charging infrastructure, typically utilizing a single source of energy. In contrast, dual-system electrification integrates multiple energy sources, such as grid power and renewable energy, enabling varied charging options. This enhances flexibility and sustainability, catering to diverse user preferences while promoting the adoption of electric vehicles in urban environments.</p></p>
