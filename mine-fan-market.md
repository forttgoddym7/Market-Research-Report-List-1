<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Fan</li><li>Medium Pressure Fan</li><li>High Pressure Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mine fan market consists of three key types based on pressure levels: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure fans are primarily used for general ventilation, offering large airflow at lower resistance. Medium pressure fans provide a balance between airflow and pressure, suitable for more demanding applications. High pressure fans generate significant airflow against higher resistance, making them essential for deep mine operations where ventilation requirements are critical. Each type plays a vital role in maintaining safe and efficient underground environments.</p></p>
