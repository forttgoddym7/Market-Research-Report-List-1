<p><p>Roof heating cables are specialized electrical wiring systems designed to prevent the accumulation of snow and ice on roofs and gutters. The growing awareness of property damage and safety concerns related to winter conditions is driving the demand for roof heating cables.</p><p>The Roof Heating Cable Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period. Trends such as the integration of smart technology for insulation management and the availability of customizable heating systems are further propelling market growth.
