<p><strong>The OLAP Database Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ROLAP</li><li>MOLAP</li><li>HOLAP</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) database systems are classified into four main types: ROLAP, MOLAP, HOLAP, and others. ROLAP (Relational OLAP) uses relational databases for data storage, allowing for scalability and complex queries. MOLAP (Multidimensional OLAP) relies on multidimensional data stores, offering faster query performance. HOLAP (Hybrid OLAP) combines the benefits of both ROLAP and MOLAP, utilizing relational databases for large data and multidimensional storage for aggregated data. Other market types may include specialized solutions tailored for specific industries or use cases.</p></p>
