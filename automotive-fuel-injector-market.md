<p><ul><li>Gasoline Port Injection</li><li>Gasoline Direct Injection</li><li>Diesel Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel injector market comprises three main types: Gasoline Port Injection, Gasoline Direct Injection, and Diesel Direct Injection. Gasoline Port Injection involves injecting fuel into the intake manifold, promoting better mixing with air and improved emissions. Gasoline Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency and power output. Diesel Direct Injection employs high-pressure systems to inject fuel directly for optimal combustion and performance, catering to the increasing demand for fuel-efficient and powerful diesel engines.</p></p>
