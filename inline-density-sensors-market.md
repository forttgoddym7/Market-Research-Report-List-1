<p><strong>The Inline Density Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline density sensors market comprises two primary types: contact type and non-contact type sensors. Contact type sensors measure density by direct interaction with the material, providing precise readings but may require frequent maintenance due to wear. Non-contact type sensors utilize technologies like electromagnetic or ultrasonic waves to assess density without physical contact, offering durability and reduced maintenance but possibly lower accuracy in certain applications. Both types play crucial roles in industrial processes, ensuring quality control and efficient operations.</p></p>
