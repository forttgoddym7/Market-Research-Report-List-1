<p><strong>The Micro Electrode Array Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Classical MEA</li><li>Multiwell-MEA</li><li>CMOS-MEA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Electrode Array (MEA) market comprises three primary types: Classical MEA, Multiwell-MEA, and CMOS-MEA. Classical MEAs are traditional configurations used for electrochemical measurements and cellular activity monitoring. Multiwell-MEAs enhance throughput by enabling simultaneous recording from multiple samples in separate wells. CMOS-MEAs integrate complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology for high-density recording and lower power consumption, improving spatial resolution and functionality. Each type serves distinct research and clinical applications, advancing neuronal and cardiac studies, drug screening, and tissue engineering.</p></p>
