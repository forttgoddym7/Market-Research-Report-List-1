<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Email management software is primarily categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based email management software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of maintenance. Users benefit from automatic updates and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. This type caters to organizations with specific regulatory requirements or those preferring to maintain their infrastructure.</p></p>
