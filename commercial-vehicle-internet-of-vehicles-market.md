<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Internet of Vehicles market encompasses various platforms that enhance connectivity and communication. </p><p>- **V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle)** platforms enable direct communication between vehicles, improving safety and traffic management.</p><p>- **V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure)** platforms link vehicles with road infrastructure, optimizing traffic flow and enabling real-time updates.</p><p>- **V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian)** platforms facilitate communication between vehicles and pedestrians, enhancing safety.</p><p>- **V2N (Vehicle-to-Network)** platforms connect vehicles to broader networks for data sharing and analysis, enabling smarter transportation systems.</p></p>
