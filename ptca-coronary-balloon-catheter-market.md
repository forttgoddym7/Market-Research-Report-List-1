Quick Exchange Type allows for quick catheter exchanges, enhancing ease of access. Overall Exchange Type enables complete catheter exchanges, facilitating prolonged interventions. Fixed Guide Wire Type ensures stability during the procedure by attaching the catheter to a guide wire. Lastly, Perfusion Type allows for continuous blood flow during balloon inflation, minimizing ischemia. Each type addresses unique challenges in coronary interventions, optimizing patient outcomes.</p></p>
