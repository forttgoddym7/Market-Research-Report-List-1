<p><p>The hernia repair products market is primarily divided into two main types: hernia mesh and mesh fixators. Hernia mesh, a prosthetic device, is used to support tissues during surgical procedures, preventing recurrences by reinforcing the weakened area. Mesh fixators, on the other hand, are tools that secure the mesh in place during the repair process. Together, these products play a crucial role in enhancing surgical outcomes, reducing recovery time, and minimizing complications associated with hernia repairs.</p></p>
