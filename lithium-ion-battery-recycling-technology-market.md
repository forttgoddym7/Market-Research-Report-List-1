<p><p>The lithium-ion battery recycling technology market encompasses various approaches, primarily focusing on cascade utilization and direct recycling. Cascade utilization involves repurposing batteries for secondary applications after their initial use, extending their life cycle and optimizing resource use. In contrast, direct recycling extracts valuable materials, such as lithium and cobalt, for use in new batteries, minimizing waste and environmental impact. Both methods aim to enhance sustainability in battery production while addressing the growing demand for energy storage solutions.</p></p>
