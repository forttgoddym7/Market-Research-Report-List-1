<p><p>The business phone systems market is primarily divided into two types: on-premise and cloud-based systems. On-premise business phone systems are installed directly at a company's location, providing greater control and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. In contrast, cloud-based business phone systems are hosted off-site and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, making them ideal for remote work environments and businesses that demand quick deployment and easy updates.</p></p>
