Fluorescence PCR utilizes fluorescent dyes to monitor the amplification process in real time, providing faster and more sensitive detection of the virus. Both types cater to different diagnostic needs in laboratories and healthcare settings, enhancing disease surveillance and outbreak response.</p></p>
