<p><p>The Ultra High Definition (UHD) panel market encompasses three primary types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD panels utilize liquid crystals and require a backlight, typically LED, to illuminate the display. LED panels are essentially LCDs with LED backlighting, offering better brightness and energy efficiency. OLED panels, on the other hand, feature organic compounds that emit light individually, resulting in superior contrast, deeper blacks, and vibrant colors. Each technology presents unique advantages, influencing consumer choice in the growing UHD market.</p></p>
