<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) market includes two main types of valves: pneumatic EGR valves and electric EGR valves. Pneumatic EGR valves operate using engine vacuum or pressure to control the flow of exhaust gas back into the intake manifold, mainly found in older vehicle models. In contrast, electric EGR valves use electronic actuators for precise control over gas recirculation, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. The evolving market trends lean towards electric valves due to increased demand for fuel efficiency and compliance with stricter emissions regulations.</p></p>
