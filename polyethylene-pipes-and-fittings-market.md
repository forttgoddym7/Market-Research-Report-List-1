<p><strong>The Polyethylene Pipes and Fittings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE)</li><li>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyethylene pipes and fittings market is categorized into various types based on density. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and suitable for low-pressure applications, while Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) offers enhanced tensile strength and elongation, making it ideal for agricultural and industrial uses. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) exhibits high strength and durability, suitable for high-pressure applications like water distribution and gas pipelines. Other types include cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) and specialized grades for specific applications, further diversifying the market.</p></p>
