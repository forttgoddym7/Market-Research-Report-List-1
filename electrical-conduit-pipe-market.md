<p><p>The electrical conduit pipe market is divided into rigid and flexible conduit pipes. Rigid conduit pipes, typically made of materials like PVC or metal, provide strong protection for electrical wiring and are ideal for permanent installations. They are resistant to physical damage and suitable for outdoor use. Conversely, flexible conduit pipes, generally made from materials like PVC or metal with a corrugated design, offer ease of installation in tight spaces and flexibility for changing layouts, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
