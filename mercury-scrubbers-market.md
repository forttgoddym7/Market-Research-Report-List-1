<p><p>The mercury scrubbers market diversifies into two main types: dry scrubbers and wet scrubbers. Dry scrubbers utilize solid materials to absorb or react with mercury emissions, allowing for particulate capture without the need for liquid mediums. In contrast, wet scrubbers use liquid solutions to remove mercury from flue gases through chemical reactions and absorption processes. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on specific applications and regulatory requirements, making them essential technologies for managing mercury pollution in various industrial settings.</p></p>
