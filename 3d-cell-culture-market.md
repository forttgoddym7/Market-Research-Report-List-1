<p><strong>The 3D Cell Culture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scaffold-based</li><li>Scaffold-free</li><li>Microchips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D cell culture market consists of three main types: scaffold-based, scaffold-free, and microchips. Scaffold-based cultures utilize biomaterials that provide structural support for cell growth, mimicking the natural extracellular matrix. Scaffold-free systems promote cell aggregation and organization without artificial supports, enhancing cell interactions and functionality. Microchips are advanced platforms that integrate microfluidics and miniaturized environments, allowing precise control over cellular conditions. Together, these approaches advance tissue engineering, drug testing, and disease modeling, fostering innovative research applications.</p></p>
