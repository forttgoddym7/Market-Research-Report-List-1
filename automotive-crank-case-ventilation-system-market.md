<p><ul><li>Natural Ventilation System</li><li>Forced Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Crank Case Ventilation System market comprises two main types: natural ventilation systems and forced ventilation systems. Natural ventilation relies on atmospheric pressure differences to allow gases to escape from the crankcase, maintaining optimal pressure. In contrast, forced ventilation systems utilize mechanical means, such as blowers or fans, to actively expel gases, enhancing efficiency and emissions control. Both systems play crucial roles in reducing harmful emissions and improving engine performance, catering to different automotive design requirements and regulations.</p></p>
