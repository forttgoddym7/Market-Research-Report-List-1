<p><p>Endpoint security and protection software can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions operate from remote servers, offering scalability, automatic updates, and accessibility across multiple devices, making them ideal for organizations with a distributed workforce. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed directly on local devices or servers, providing greater control over security policies and data management. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific security requirements, infrastructure, and compliance needs.</p></p>
