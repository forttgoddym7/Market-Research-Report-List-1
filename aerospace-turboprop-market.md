<p><p>Aerospace turboprops are a type of aircraft propulsion system that combines the efficiency of a turbine engine with the performance of a propeller. Additionally, advancements in turboprop engine technology, such as greater thrust-to-weight ratios and enhanced reliability, are expected to drive market growth. </p><p>Key trends in the market include the introduction of hybrid-electric turboprop systems, addressing environmental concerns and emissions regulations.
