<p><p>The Human Machine Interface (HMI) software market is categorized into three main types. Supervisory Level HMI focuses on monitoring and controlling processes from a higher organizational level, facilitating data analysis and decision-making. Machine Level HMI, in contrast, operates directly on individual machines, providing real-time control and feedback to operators. The "Others" category encompasses specialized or hybrid solutions that may integrate features from both supervisory and machine levels, catering to specific industry needs or functionalities.</p></p>
