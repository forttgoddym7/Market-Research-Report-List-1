<p><p>The Dual Crystal Straight Probe market can be categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact probes operate by directly touching the surface of the material being tested, providing accurate measurements of properties like thickness or surface uniformity. Non-contact probes, on the other hand, use technologies such as laser or ultrasound to assess material characteristics without physical contact, allowing for the inspection of delicate or hazardous materials. Both types serve vital roles in industrial applications for quality control and material analysis.</p></p>
