<p><p>The wrapping tissue market comprises three main types: virgin pulp, de-inked pulp, and mixed pulp. Virgin pulp is sourced directly from trees, offering high quality and strength, ideal for premium applications. De-inked pulp is derived from recycled paper, processed to remove inks and contaminants, making it a sustainable option. Mixed pulp combines both virgin and recycled materials, balancing cost-effectiveness with quality. Each type serves various consumer needs, impacting performance, environmental sustainability, and pricing in the wrapping tissue sector.</p></p>
