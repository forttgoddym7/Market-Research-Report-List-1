<p><p>The bilirubin meter for infants is designed to measure bilirubin levels, which are crucial for diagnosing jaundice. The market is primarily divided into two types: transcutaneous and bench-top meters. Transcutaneous bilirubin meters use light reflection to assess skin pigment levels non-invasively, providing quick results. In contrast, bench-top bilirubin meters require a blood sample for more precise readings. Both types are essential in neonatal care, helping healthcare professionals monitor and manage jaundice effectively in newborns.</p></p>
