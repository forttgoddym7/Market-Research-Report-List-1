<p><p>Soy protein powder, particularly in the form of soy peptide powder, is categorized into two primary market types: food grade and raw material grade. Food grade soy protein powder is specifically processed and suitable for human consumption, often used in protein supplements, snacks, and alternative meat products. In contrast, raw material grade soy protein powder is primarily intended for industrial applications, such as animal feed or ingredient manufacturing, and may not meet the stringent safety and purity standards required for food products.</p></p>
